If you don't understand what RSS is, you can learn how to use RSS.

There are two ways to start following a comic strip. The first way is to copy an RSS link by clicking the RSS icon and then pasting it into your favorite feed reader. If you use Feedly, then you can just click the Feedly icon to view the feed, and then click Follow in Feedly.
